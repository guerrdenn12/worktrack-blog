FAQ

Common questions

Do employees need to download anything from the App Store? +
No App Store needed. Employees open your app's URL in their phone browser and tap "Add to Home Screen." It installs like a native app with an icon. Works on iPhone (Safari) and Android (Chrome).
Is there a monthly fee after I buy? +
No. WorkTrack is a one-time purchase. Hosting is on Netlify's free tier (which handles thousands of users). Email notifications use EmailJS free tier (200 emails/month). There are zero ongoing costs unless you outgrow the free tiers.
How does data get saved? Is it secure? +
Data is stored in the browser's local storage on the device you use to manage the app. Manager access is protected by a PIN. Employee access is individual per person. You can download a full JSON backup anytime from Settings. For multi-device cloud sync, a Supabase backend upgrade is available.
Can I edit employee time entries? +
Yes. Managers can edit any time entry — change the timestamp, add a note — and those edits are flagged in the log with an "Edited" marker so there's always a clear audit trail. You can also add manual entries for employees who forgot to clock in.
How do email notifications work? +
Emails are sent using EmailJS, a free service that lets you send emails without a backend server. You create a free account at emailjs.com, set up a template with your wording, and paste your credentials into WorkTrack settings. Full step-by-step setup is included in the README.
How many employees can I have? +
There's no hard limit. WorkTrack is built for small businesses, so it works great up to about 50 employees. Beyond that, performance may vary depending on the device being used for management. For larger teams, a backend database upgrade is available.
